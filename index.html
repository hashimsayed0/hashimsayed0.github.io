Biriyani

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 1 Kg
Salt 1 tbsp
Ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
Red chilli paste 1 tbsp

How to Make Hyderabadi Biryani

  1. Clean the meat. Now in a pan add meat, salt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, green chilli paste, sauteed brown onions, cardamom powder, cinnamon, cumin seeds, cloves, mace, mint leaves and lemon juice.
  2. Mix all of these ingredients thoroughly together.
  3. Add curd, clarified butter, semi cooked rice, saffron, water and oil. Mix it well.
  4. Now apply sticky dough on the sides of the pan.
  5. Cover with lid to seal it and cook for about 25 minutes.
  6. Hyderabadi Biryani is ready to eat. Garnish it with boiled eggs, sliced carrots, cucumbers and serve hot.

Bajra-Methi Missi Roti

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bajra flour 200 Gram
Wheat flour 100 Gram
Fenugreek leaves 250 Gram
Garlic cloves, finely chopped 2

How to Make Bajra-Methi Missi Roti

  1. Combine all the ingredients and knead into firm dough with required amount of water.
  2. Divide into five portions. Roll out into thin rotis and roast on a tawa till they are well done.
  3. Serve hot with green chutney.

Valencia Fizz

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Valencia orange juice 100 Ml
Soda 75 Ml
Lemon slices 1
Lemon juice 2 Tbsp

How to Make Valencia Fizz

  1. Take a mocktail glass and first add valencia orange juice.
  2. Add sugar and lemon juice.
  3. Add soda and stir it well.
  4. Deck up the lemon slice on the top of the glass for better presentation.
